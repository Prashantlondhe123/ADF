Activity:

    Activities are the GUI widgets within Data 
Factory that do specific kinds of data 
movement or transformation. There is a
CopyData activity to move data, a ForEach 
activity to loop over a file list, a Filter 
activity that chooses a subset of files, etc. 
Most activities have a source and a sink
